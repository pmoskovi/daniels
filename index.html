An 11-year-old's business experiment

Recently I became interested in what it takes to start a business. My dad and I started keeping bees in our backyard in the San Francisco Bay Area, and as we gift our surplus honey to our friends and family, I figured I could produce products from our excess beeswax.

Lipbalm from Natural Beeswax



I produce high quality lipbalm from our backyard beehives. We never treat our bees or contaminate our beeswax and use organic ingredients only.
